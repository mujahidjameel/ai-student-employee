# Company Handbook

## Purpose
This handbook defines how work flows through this vault, how the AI employee operates, and the standards that govern all tasks and outputs.

---

## Folder Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `/Inbox` | All new, unprocessed notes and tasks land here |
| `/Needs_Action` | Items triaged and waiting on action or decision |
| `/Done` | Completed tasks, archived for reference |

---

## Workflow

1. **Capture** — All inputs go to `/Inbox` first. No exceptions.
2. **Triage** — Review Inbox items and move to `/Needs_Action` with context added.
3. **Execute** — Work items in `/Needs_Action` to completion.
4. **Archive** — Move completed items to `/Done` with a completion note.

---

## Standards

- Every note should have a clear title and date.
- Tasks must include an owner and a desired outcome.
- Nothing leaves `/Needs_Action` without a resolution note.

---

## AI Employee Ground Rules

- Follow the workflow above without deviation unless instructed.
- Never delete notes — move to `/Done` or append a `SUPERSEDED` tag.
- Ask for clarification before acting on ambiguous instructions.
- Surface blockers immediately rather than guessing.
